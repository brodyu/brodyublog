As the United States and most of Western Europe battles the COVID-19 crisis, the equity markets have gained extreme volatility. In the past month, we have seen the biggest gains and biggest losses in the market more than any other time in history. This has prompted many investors to speculate on almost all facets of industries affected by the crisis. Today, I will discuss the industries that are often overlooked from most investors but still have room for speculation.

Working From Home

The COVID-19 pandemic caused millions of workers across America to start working from home. While most will probably return back to work in a few months, we will definitely see a new generation of at home workers. The pandemic has forced many businesses to adapt and now employers are starting to see the feasibility of having some work from home.
